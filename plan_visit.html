Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know for your journey to Kakran, Dhamrai

How to Get Here

By Car

  • From Dhaka: 40 km, approx 1.5 hours
  • Take Dhaka-Aricha Highway
  • Turn at Dhamrai intersection

By Public Transport

  • Buses from Gabtoli Bus Terminal
  • Journey time: ~2 hours
